Heavy Industry Pegs

Heavy Industry Pegs provide sturdy, solid support for any type of rider. Sized to offer ideal surface area for confident control, with splined adapters that allow the footpeg angle to be dialed in for optimum comfort. Premium rubber reduces harsh road vibrations and surrounds a knurled aluminum center for overall improved traction. Rugged, industrial styling coordinates with the entire Heavy Industry collection of products.

  • Cast from quality aluminum with premium rubber compound
  • Choose from Chrome or Satin Black finishes
  • Available with included male mount adapters for direct replacement on most H-D models
  • Heavy Industry Pegs without adapters are for non-H-D applications and require model-specific adapters (sold separately) for fitment on a wide range of makes and models

Heavy Industry Grips

Don't be fooled by the rugged appearance of Heavy Industry Grips. The proprietary EPDM rubber compound provides a soft, vibration-dampening feel for long-haul comfort, while the tough industrial styling hides your softer side.

  • Housings are made from quality cast aluminum with form-fitted premium EPDM rubber compound for comfort, durability and longevity
  • Knurled areas between rubber pads provide both style and added grip
  • EPDM rubber pads dampen vibration
  • Includes matching aluminum end caps

Heavy Industry Driver Floorboards

Kuryakyn Heavy Industry Driver Boards are designed for long-haul comfort with rugged styling and durable construction. The aluminum boards feature an industrial-themed design with a knurled center surface incorporated into the top of the housing and vibration-dampening soft rubber inserts for comfort and traction. Installation is a breeze reusing the existing hardware.

Heavy Industry Shift Pegs

Heavy Industry Shift Pegs are built to handle whatever the road throws their way. A direct replacement for stock H-D shift pegs with rugged style and durable construction. Premium rubber frames the outer perimeter for reduced vibration and improved control, while the knurled aluminum center provides traction for your boots.

  • Cast from quality aluminum with premium EPDM rubber
  • Easy to install, direct replacement for factory H-D shift pegs
  • Available in Chrome or Satin Black
  • Coordinated style with the complete line of Kuryakyn Heavy Industry accessories

Heavy Industry Brake Pedal Pegs

Heavy Industry Brake Pedal Pads are the epitome of durability, style and function. A premium rubber insert exposes socket head fasteners as it wraps around the knurled aluminum center for improved traction and a rugged, contrasting appearance.

  • Cast from quality aluminum with premium rubber compound for reduced vibration
  • Knurled aluminum center offers improved traction and control
  • Easy installation, with applications for Harley-Davidson FL, XG and select FX Softail models with forward controls
  • Available in Chrome or Satin Black finishes
  • Perfect addition to the Heavy Industry Floorboards (P/N 7041 Chrome, 7042 Satin Black), or Heavy Industry Footpegs (P/N 7031 Chrome, 7032 Satin Black)

Heavy Industry Mirrors

The rugged and industrial design of our Heavy Industry Mirrors is ideal for minimalist riders seeking gritty style paired with pure form and function.

  • Offered in four finish configurations: Chrome, Satin Black, Chrome with Satin Black, and Satin Black with Chrome
  • Additional Stem and Accents Kits sold separately for a variety of customizing options
  • Mirror heads measure approximately 6" long x 3" wide
  • Stems are 6" long and position mirror heads 4" above and 5-1/2" outward from mounting location
  • Includes mounting hardware for H-D applications, adapters for other applications sold separately
  • Styling coordinates with the entire line of Kuryakyn Heavy Industry parts and accessories
